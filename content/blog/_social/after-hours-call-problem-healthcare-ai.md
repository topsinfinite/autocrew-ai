The numbers stack up faster than most owners realize:

- Traditional answering services charge $150–$400/month and don't book — just take messages

The fix isn't a bigger answering service contract. It's a phone system that books appointments in real time, captures intake data in a HIPAA-aware environment, and escalates emergencies to your on-call provider — without needing a human to be awake at 11pm on a Tuesday.

For HIPAA: PHI redaction in logs, BAA-eligible infrastructure, encrypted storage. Non-negotiable for any vendor handling patient data.
