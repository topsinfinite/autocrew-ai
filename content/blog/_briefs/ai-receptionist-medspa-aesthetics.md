## Why this topic

Medspas have higher CAC than dental, longer consult-to-book cycles, and callers who actively shop multiple clinics on the same call. Existing competitor coverage (Zenoti, Spa Voices, MySalonDesk, AnswerBug, JustCall) is mostly point-solution oriented. Autocrew can frame as a multi-step **consult-to-treatment booking flow** rather than a "answer the phone" feature.

## AEO summary draft

Medspas convert at the lowest rate per inbound call of any service vertical because their callers shop 3–5 clinics on the same call. An AI receptionist that qualifies on the first call, books a consult directly, and follows up with pre-treatment paperwork closes the multi-step gap that loses customers between "I'll call back" and "I'll go with someone else." For a medspa seeing 30–50 daily inquiries, recapturing 25–30% of those translates to tens of thousands in monthly retained revenue.

## Competitive gap

Most medspa AI vendors stop at "we book treatments." None walk through the consult-to-treatment funnel as a multi-step flow with AI at each stage. That framing makes the post a buyer's guide, not a feature pitch.
