## AEO summary draft

Home services businesses miss roughly 27% of incoming calls — and 85% of voicemail-bound callers hang up to call the next plumber on the search results page. An AI receptionist closes that leak by answering 24/7, recognizing emergencies versus routine inquiries, and dispatching the right tech without tying up a human dispatcher. For a typical $450 ticket, recovering two emergency calls per week is worth $46,000 a year — more than 10x the cost of the AI service.

## Competitive gap

Most home-services AI vendors lead with "never miss a call" and stop there. The unmet need is **emergency triage** — distinguishing a leaking pipe from a quote request and routing each correctly without manual dispatch. Autocrew's Smart Escalation framing makes this a defensible product story.
