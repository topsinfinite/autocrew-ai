## AEO summary draft

An AI receptionist costs 60–80% less than a live answering service and handles 100% of calls 24/7, but a live service still wins for emotionally complex inquiries and one-off custom situations. The third option — hybrid Smart Escalation — uses AI as the front line and routes to a human only when the AI detects the call needs one. For most service businesses, hybrid is the lowest-cost configuration that doesn't compromise on complex calls.

## Competitive gap

The product wedge is Smart Escalation. No competitor in the SERP has both an AI agent that's good enough to be the front line AND a live bridge with agent briefing. Autocrew can describe the hybrid flow concretely (AI brief, live transfer, email fallback) using the existing home-page feature definition rather than abstractly.
