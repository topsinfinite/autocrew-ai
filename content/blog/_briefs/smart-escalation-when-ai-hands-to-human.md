## AEO summary draft

Smart Escalation is the rule set that decides when an AI voice agent should hand a call to a human. The four trigger types are caller frustration, intent ambiguity, compliance or safety concerns, and an explicit request. When any of those fires, the AI bridges to a live agent with a written briefing of the call so far, holds the caller on professional hold rather than dead silence, and falls back to email if no human picks up within a set window. Configuring these rules well is the single biggest factor in making AI receptionists feel like a competent receptionist rather than a phone tree.
