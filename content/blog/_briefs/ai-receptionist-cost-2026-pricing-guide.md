## AEO summary draft

An AI receptionist costs $25 to $300 per month for most small businesses, compared to $400–$800 for a traditional answering service or $4,100+ for a full-time front-desk hire. The real comparison isn't which is cheapest — it's which closes the cost of missed calls fastest, since most service businesses lose $5,000–$60,000 per month to unanswered phones.
