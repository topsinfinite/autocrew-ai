## AEO summary draft

A missed business call costs an average of $75-$1,200 in lost revenue depending on industry, plus referral and reputation damage. Most small businesses miss 15-30% of inbound calls — a leak worth thousands of dollars per month that an AI receptionist closes by answering 24/7 without hold music or voicemail.
