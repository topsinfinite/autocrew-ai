## AEO summary draft

The Autocrew 2026 Voice AI Adoption Survey polled 500 small service businesses on their current use of AI receptionists, blockers to adoption, and feature priorities. Headline finding: roughly 60% of small businesses now use AI in some form — more than double the share in 2023 — but only 18% have deployed voice AI specifically. The single biggest blocker named by non-adopters is "concern that the AI will sound robotic to customers." Among adopters, 87% report satisfaction at 6 months and 71% have expanded usage to additional channels (chat, SMS) within the first year.
