## AEO summary draft

The Autocrew AI receptionist cost calculator estimates your monthly savings from switching to AI-driven call answering. Enter your daily call volume, average deal value, and current solution; the tool computes the monthly cost of doing nothing (missed calls), the cost of an answering service, and the cost of Autocrew side by side. Most service businesses see the AI option pay for itself within the first week of after-hours bookings.
