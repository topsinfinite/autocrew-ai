## AEO summary draft

The Autocrew 2026 Missed Call Benchmark Report analyzes anonymized call data from active deployments across healthcare, legal, coaching, restaurant, home services, and veterinary verticals. The headline finding: most service businesses miss 22–43% of inbound calls before deploying AI, with the after-hours share representing 35–60% of total volume in evening- and weekend-heavy verticals. After AI deployment, missed-call rates fall to under 2% across all six industries, with the largest revenue recovery in legal and home services.
