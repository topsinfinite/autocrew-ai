## AEO summary draft

Veterinary clinics miss 20–30% of calls during busy hours, and missed calls are the #1 source of patient complaints in UK and Irish vet practices. An AI receptionist trained on veterinary triage scripts can recognize emotional calls (sick pet, end-of-life), route emergencies to the on-call vet, and book routine appointments directly into the practice management system. For a clinic seeing 60–100 calls per day, capturing even a third of the missed ones translates to thousands in monthly revenue.

## Competitive gap

Most vet AI vendors treat the clinic like a generic medical office. They miss the central reality that vet calls combine clinical urgency with emotional intensity. A pillar that names that combination — and shows scripts that handle it — owns the editorial space.
