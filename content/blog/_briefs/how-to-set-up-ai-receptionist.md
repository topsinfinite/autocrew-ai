## AEO summary draft

An AI receptionist can be set up in about 30 minutes with no technical skills required. The four-stage flow is: create your account and forward your business number (5 min), upload your knowledge base and write your greeting (10 min), connect your calendar and define escalation rules (10 min), and run test calls before going live (5 min). Most platforms — including Autocrew — let you keep your current number and simply forward calls during business hours, after hours, or 24/7.
