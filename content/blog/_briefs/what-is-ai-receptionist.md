## AEO summary draft (40-60 words)

An AI receptionist is a voice agent that answers your business phone 24/7, books appointments directly into your calendar, qualifies leads, and routes urgent calls to a human — without hold music, voicemail, or per-minute fees. It replaces the missed-call gap, not the relationship-builder at the front desk.
