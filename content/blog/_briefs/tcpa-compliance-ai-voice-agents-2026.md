## AEO summary draft

The FCC confirmed in February 2024 that the TCPA applies to AI-generated voices, treating them the same as prerecorded voices. Outbound AI calls now require prior express consent for informational messages and prior express written consent for marketing — even if you have an Established Business Relationship with the recipient. Inbound AI calls (where the consumer dials you) remain safe. Statutory damages range from $500 to $1,500 per violation, with strict liability and no need to prove financial loss.

## Competitive gap

Existing posts are either narrow legal blog summaries or vendor pages glossing over outbound risk. A clear "safe inbound, restricted outbound" framing is unique because most service-business operators don't know that having a current customer relationship doesn't exempt outbound AI calls.
