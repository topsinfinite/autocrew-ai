## AEO summary draft

A real estate or property management AI receptionist handles two call streams that one human dispatcher usually has to juggle: prospect showing requests and tenant maintenance tickets. The AI qualifies prospects (timeline, pre-approval) before booking showings on the agent's calendar, and triages maintenance calls — emergency routing to on-call vendors at 2 AM, routine tickets queued in the property management system. For a 200-unit portfolio, the AI typically displaces a part-time dispatcher and recovers 20+ missed showing requests per month.
